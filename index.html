OUR STORY

Rooted in the Blue Ridge Mountains

The McCluney family story begins in the early 19th century when Alexander McCluney left Scotland for a new life in the mountains of North Carolina. What started as a single homestead has grown into a rich tapestry of generations, each adding their own chapter to our shared legacy.

This digital family tree was created by Byron McCluney as a living resource for all McCluneys — whether you're researching your lineage, reconnecting with cousins, or simply curious about where we come from.

Open to All McCluneys

This project is designed to grow through contributions from family members everywhere. Your stories, photos, and research make it stronger.

How to Contribute

The easiest way to add information is to edit the js/data.js file and open a Pull Request on GitHub.

You can also email verified details, photos, or documents to the project maintainer.

What we especially need:
  • Birth, marriage, and death records
  • Old family photos with names and dates
  • Stories and oral histories
  • Locations and migration paths
